Shri Konidela Pawankalyan

Konidela Pawan Kalyan is an Indian politician, actor, serving as the 11th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024.

About

Konidela Pawan Kalyan born on 2 September 1971. He is also the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development and Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forest, Science and Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh as MLA representing the Pithapuram constituency. He is the founder and president of the Janasena Party. He is knownly called as 'POWERSTAR'.
